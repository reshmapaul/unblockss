Who benefits from better patient-directed access?

Take a look at the questions below.

Anything sound familiar?

Health Information Management professionals (HIMs)

  • Does your organization embrace and comply with regulatory mandates?
  • Do you know the organizational barriers patients face when accessing their info?
  • Does your team have a strategy to support patient-directed access?
  • Do you still process hand-written or faxed addendum requests?

Office of Patient Experience

  • Does poor coordination harm patient outcomes and satisfaction?
  • Do access delays diminish HCAPS scores or facility and caregiver reputations?
  • Would easier online access attract patients to receive care from your organization?

Health Information Exchanges (HIEs) & Qualified Health Information Networks (QHINs)

  • Is supporting patient-directed access a priority for your organization?
  • Do you lack subject matter expertise about individual access services?
  • Are you ready for a streamlined approach to getting patients the info they need?

Patients, carepartners, caregivers, advocates

  • Are you facing a life-altering diagnosis without fast access to your medical information?
  • Does “information blocking” hinder your understanding and care decisions?
  • Do you need to correct an error or omission in your medical record?
